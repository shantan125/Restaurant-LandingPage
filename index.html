Namaste and Merhaba!,

I'm thrilled to welcome you to TandoorTreats, a culinary haven where the rich flavors of India and the vibrant tastes of Turkey come together in perfect harmony. As the proud owner, I've always been passionate about bringing people together through the joy of food, and TandoorTreats is the culmination of that dream.

Our Story

Inspired by the bustling streets of Delhi and the enchanting markets of Istanbul, TandoorTreats is a celebration of the diverse culinary traditions that make India and Turkey truly special. Our journey began with a simple idea - to create a space where you can experience the authentic taste of chicken shawarma and indulge in mouthwatering desserts, all infused with the warmth and spices of India.

What Sets Us Apart

At TandoorTreats, we take pride in crafting every dish with love and attention to detail. Our chicken shawarmas are seasoned to perfection, slow-cooked to juicy tenderness, and served with a variety of delectable toppings. From the traditional tandoor to the fragrant spices of Turkish cuisine, each bite is a flavorful adventure. But TandoorTreats is not just about savory delights; our dessert corner is a sweet escape. Dive into the world of heavenly treats, from the decadent Gulab Jamun to the irresistible Baklava. Every dessert is a journey through the rich cultural tapestry of India and Turkey.

Our Promise

Here at TandoorTreats, we are committed to providing a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every guest feels like a part of our extended family. Whether you're craving a quick bite or looking for a place to celebrate, we invite you to savor the essence of India and Turkey in every dish we serve.
